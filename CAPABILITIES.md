# What your agent can do

Your agent is a browser chat that speaks in your voice (from `PERSONALITY.md`).
On top of that, you add **powers** — ready-made modules and connectors that let it
**automate your everyday workflows**. Here's the full menu. You choose which to add.

---

## 🧠 Memory
1. **Remember what you tell it** — facts, preferences, people, decisions — across chats.
2. **Recall relevant context automatically** before every reply, so it never starts cold.
3. **Answer "what did we discuss about X?"** from your own history.
4. **Get sharper over time** — useful memories are kept, noise is forgotten.

## 📧 Email (Gmail)
5. **Summarize your unread inbox** — a one-glance digest of who wants what.
6. **Draft replies in your voice** — ready-to-review drafts (never auto-sent).
7. **Triage the inbox** — separate real human mail from no-reply/marketing noise.
8. **Draft a single reply on demand** — paste a message, get a polished reply.

## ⏰ Reminders & scheduling
9. **One-off reminders/messages** — "send me a hi at 7:30pm."
10. **Recurring reminders** — "every weekday at 9am, nudge me about standup."
11. **Scheduled messages to a channel** — post something later (with Slack added).

## 📅 Calendar
12. **Block time** — "block 6–8pm today for deep work."
13. **Check availability** — "am I free Thursday afternoon?"
14. **Create events** — "add a call with Sam tomorrow at 4pm."

## 💬 Slack
15. **Reply to DMs/mentions** in your voice, in-thread.
16. **Summarize a busy thread or channel** — catch you up.
17. **Send a message on demand** — "post 'standup in 5' to #general."
18. **Edit or delete** a message it sent.

## 🔗 Combined (powers working together)
19. **Read an email → summarize → remember** the key facts for later.
20. **Recall context to write sharper replies** across email and Slack.
21. **Scheduled daily digest** — each morning, summarize unread email (+ Slack) and send it to you.

---

*Safe by design:* email is read-only (it drafts, never sends); the Slack bot only
edits/deletes its **own** messages; memory is a local, rebuildable index. You stay
in control — your agent prepares, you decide.
